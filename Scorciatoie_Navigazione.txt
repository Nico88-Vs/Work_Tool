- **Go to Everything/Type**: `Ctrl+T`
- **Go to File**: `Ctrl+Shift+T`
- **Go to Symbol**: `Alt+\`
- **Find Usages**: `Shift+F12`
- **Go to Declaration**: `Ctrl+B` o `F12`
- **Go to Implementation**: `Ctrl+Alt+B`
- **Go to Base**: `Alt+Home`
- **Go to Derived Symbols**: `Alt+End`
- **Go to Next/Previous Member**: `Alt+Up` o `Alt+Down`
- **Go to Related Files**: `Alt+Shift+L`
- **Go to Containing Declaration**: `Ctrl+[`
